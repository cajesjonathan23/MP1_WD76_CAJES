Virgin Island

Pungtud Island, also known as Virgin Island, is essentially a sandbar with palm trees. It’s approximately 15 minutes by boat from Panglao Island

BALICASAG ISLAND

People go to Balicasag to visit the popular fish sanctuary and turtle areas, either for snorkeling or diving.

DOLJO BEACH

Doljo Beach is in East Panglao 15 kilometers far from Alona Beach. It’s a beautiful beach with white sand and quiet. But they aren’t taking care of it and is little bit dirty of trash.

PAMILACAN ISLAND

Pamilacan Island is located in South Bohol is around one hour by boat from Baclayon. It’s great for snorkeling and you can stay overnight if you wish.

BAGOBO BEACH

Bagobo Beach is close to Dumaluan Beach. It’s close to Arco point one of the most popular diving points in Panglao Island. If you like snorkeling in Bohol beaches get your snorkeling mask and have an amazing swim around Bagobo Beach.

Tableya Chocolate

This sweet Bohol delicacy comprises ground cacao beans formed into small, round balls.

Broas or Ladyfingers

Ladyfingers, locally known as broas and more famous in Baclayon, Bohol, are crispy yet lightweight delicacies that excite your mouth.
